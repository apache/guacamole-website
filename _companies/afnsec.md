AFNSec is a cybersecurity company providing managed cyber defense, vulnerability management, and cyber training to help organizations reduce risk and improve resilience. Secure Access Portal is built on Apache Guacamole and delivers enterprise grade access controls, auditing, deployment, customization, and operational support for secure remote access environments across Africa.
